About Us - Alba Rapids

Our mission is to provide unforgettable white water rafting experiences while prioritizing safety and environmental stewardship. We believe every adventure should be thrilling, safe, and respectful of nature.

History

Alba Rapids was founded in 2015 by Gabriel Alba, a passionate river guide with over 15 years of experience navigating challenging waters.

What started as weekend trips with friends grew into a full-service rafting company dedicated to sharing the thrill of white water adventures with everyone.
